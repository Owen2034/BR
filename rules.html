Server Rules

1. General Behaviour

  • Respect Everyone – No racism, sexism, homophobia, or discrimination of any kind.
  • No Toxicity – Harassment, bullying, or endless arguing is forbidden.
  • Common Sense Applies – If it feels like you're pushing boundaries, chances are you are.

2. Roleplay Standards

  • Stay In Character (IC) – Always remain in character when within the city.
  • No Random Deathmatch (RDM) – Do not attack or kill others for no RP reason.
  • No Vehicle Deathmatch (VDM) – Don't drive into or kill others with vehicles unless RP-justified.
  • Powergaming is Prohibited – Don't push something on somebody else or utilize impossible means to win.
  • Metagaming is Prohibited – Don't use OOC information (Discord, streams, etc.) in character.

3. Criminal RP

  • Be Realistic – Don't fall from 10 stories or engage in a shootout in the middle of crowded areas with no regard for consequences.
  • Priority Rules – Respect active priority rules. If staff say "cooldown", respect it.
  • Hostage Situations & Robberies – Must be well-planned. Unrealistic demands or system exploitation is not allowed.
  • Police Interactions – You must fear the law in a realistic sense. Don't make every arrest a shootout.

4. Police / UHS / UKFB

  • Stay Professional – If you're in a whitelisted career (Police, UHS, UKFB), you must be mature and realistic.
  • Chain of Command – Listen to your superiors and follow your department's SOPs.
  • Corruption RP – Approval from staff must be obtained prior to any corrupt activity being roleplayed.

5. Combat & Weapons

  • Gunplay Follows RP – RP must come before shooting. Be serious about weapons.
  • Weapons Limitations – You must roleplay obtaining illegal weapons and keeping them in a secure manner.
  • Fear RP – If you're outgunned or being held hostage, be frightened. Don't try to be a hero.

6. Driving & Vehicles

  • Drive Realistically – Handle vehicles the way you would in the real world; no superhuman stunts.
  • No Unrealistic Vehicles – No modded vehicle abuse, invisible parts, or physics exploits.
  • Parking & Storage – Do not abuse garages, impound, or vehicle respawn mechanics.

7. Assets & Economy

  • No Exploiting – Any form of script exploiting will result in an instant ban.
  • Don't Hoard – Store your assets as believable for the character's plot and profession.
